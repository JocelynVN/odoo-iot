Objectives & Key Results - OKRs

OKR (Objectives & Key Results) is a goal-setting framework that defines and connects enterprise, department, and employee objectives to specific outcomes. OKRs assist enterprises in concentrating on critical goals, pooling all resources to achieve them. They also aid individuals in understanding their role in the shared goal and measuring all activities. This application helps you focus on your objectives and evaluate them based on key results.
